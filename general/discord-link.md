# Discord Link

Once linked, players gain access to exclusive features, role synchronization and improved community integration.

***

### Benefits of Linking Your Accounts

By linking your Minecraft and Discord accounts, you will unlock the following advantages:

* 🎖 **Exclusive Free Badge**\
  Receive a special badge that is only available to players who link their accounts.
* 🔗 **Rank Synchronization**\
  Your Minecraft ranks are automatically linked with matching Discord roles.
* ✅ **Account Verification**\
  Prove ownership of your Minecraft account on Discord.

***

### How to Link/Unlink Your Accounts

Linking or unlinking your accounts only takes a few moments.

1. Join the Minecraft server.
2. Use the command `/discord <link or unlink>` in-game. (Only works in lobby)
3. Follow the instructions shown in chat.
4. Once completed, your accounts will be linked automatically.

***

### Rank Synchronization

Once your accounts are linked:

* Minecraft ranks are automatically assigned as Discord roles
* Removing or changing a rank updates your Discord roles accordingly

> ⚠ Rank synchronization may require you to rejoin or wait a few seconds for updates.

***

### Notes

* Each Minecraft account can only be linked to **one Discord account**
* Linking is optional&#x20;
* Staff will never ask for your password
