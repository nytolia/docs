### How to Link/Unlink Your Accounts

Linking or unlinking your accounts only takes a few moments.

1. Join the Minecraft server.
2. Use the command `/discord <link or unlink>` in-game. (Only works in lobby)
3. Follow the instructions shown in chat.
4. Once completed, your accounts will be linked automatically.

***

### Rank & Role Synchronization

Once your accounts are linked:

* Minecraft ranks are automatically assigned as Discord roles
* Removing or changing a rank updates your Discord roles accordingly
