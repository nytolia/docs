# 🧩 Origin World

The Origin World is the heart of the server. Unlike other worlds, the origin world is a adventure world to complete quests, go on dungeons and get in the storyline.

It is a land of floating islands, serving as the bridge between all known worlds.

***

### Aurelius: The Rift Guardian

Aurelius is an ancient old man tasked with maintaining the balance between dimensions. He serves as the Primary Teleportation Hub for the server. By interacting with him, players can access the various Rifts that lead to other worlds.

#### Navigation Menu

When speaking to Aurelius, you can choose from the following destinations:

1. The Resource World
2. The Islands

***

### Adventures & Dungeons

The Origin World isn't just a lobby; it's a springboard for action.

* NPC Quests: Many NPCs in the Origin World have tasks that require you to travel to other dimensions and return with specific trophies.
* The Dungeon Gate: Located just behind Aestalgard, there are more cities to explore and get into dungeons!
