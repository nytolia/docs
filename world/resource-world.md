# 📦 Resource World

#### 🗺️ Overview

The Resource World is a shared. Unlike your personal island, this world is temporary. To keep the landscape fresh and the ores plentiful, the entire dimension is reset on a regular schedule.

* Purpose: Gathering raw materials (Wood, Stone, Ores, Crops) to build on your own island.
* Reset Cycle: Every Sunday at 12:00 AM Server Time.
* Difficulty: Normal (Hunger and mobs are active).

***

#### 🪓 Resources

While you can find almost everything here like in minecraft, players primarily visit for the following quests:

| Resource    | Best Biome            | Common Uses                                      |
| ----------- | --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| Wood        | Forests / Jungles     | Building blocks, charcoal, and early-game tools. |
| Stone/Ores  | Extreme Hills / Caves | Upgrading gear and island structures.            |
| Sand/Gravel | Deserts / Rivers      | Glass production and concrete for modern builds. |
